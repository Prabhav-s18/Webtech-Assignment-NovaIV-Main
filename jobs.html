Front-End Developer

Reference Number: NV001

Salary: $75,000 – $90,000 AUD per annum

Reports To: Head of Digital Products

Employment Type: Full-Time

About the Role

We are looking for a skilled Front-End Developer to join our e-commerce team at NovaIV Cameras. You will be responsible for building and maintaining the customer-facing product pages, shopping cart experience, and overall website performance. You will work closely with our UX and back-end teams to deliver a seamless online shopping experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain responsive web pages using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Optimise website performance and page load speeds for improved user experience
  • Collaborate with designers to translate mockups into pixel-perfect interfaces
  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility across Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge
  • Implement accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1) across all web pages

Essential Requirements

  1. Minimum 2 years of professional front-end development experience
  2. Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript
  3. Experience with responsive design and mobile-first development
  4. Familiarity with version control using Git and GitHub
  5. Understanding of web accessibility guidelines (WCAG)

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience with e-commerce platforms such as Shopify or WooCommerce
  • Knowledge of CSS frameworks or preprocessors (SASS/LESS)
  • Exposure to React or Vue.js
  • Personal interest in photography or camera equipment

Back-End Developer

Reference Number: NV002

Salary: $85,000 – $100,000 AUD per annum

Reports To: Head of Digital Products

Employment Type: Full-Time

About the Role

NovaIV Cameras is seeking an experienced Back-End Developer to support the infrastructure behind our online retail platform. You will design, build, and maintain server-side logic, databases, and APIs that power the NovaIV shopping experience for thousands of customers across Australia.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement RESTful APIs to support front-end features
  • Manage and optimise relational databases (MySQL or PostgreSQL)
  • Integrate third-party services such as payment gateways and shipping APIs
  • Ensure application security, including data validation and protection against common vulnerabilities
  • Monitor server performance and troubleshoot production issues

Essential Requirements

  1. Minimum 3 years of back-end development experience
  2. Proficiency in at least one server-side language (Python, Node.js, or PHP)
  3. Strong understanding of database design and SQL
  4. Experience building and consuming RESTful APIs
  5. Knowledge of web security best practices

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS or Google Cloud
  • Familiarity with Docker or containerisation tools
  • Exposure to agile or scrum development methodologies
  • Experience working in an e-commerce or retail environment

Camera Specialist

Reference Number: NV004

Salary: $58,000 – $70,000 AUD per annum

Reports To: Retail Operations Manager

Employment Type: Full-Time / Part-Time Available

About the Role

As a Camera Specialist at NovaIV Cameras, you will be the go-to expert for our online customers and internal teams. You will produce product reviews, assist customers with technical queries, and help curate our product catalogue to ensure accuracy and quality across all listings.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide expert product advice to customers via live chat, email, and phone
  • Write accurate and informative product descriptions and buying guides
  • Test and review new camera products before they are listed on the site
  • Assist the merchandising team with product categorisation and tagging
  • Train other staff on camera products, terminology, and features

Essential Requirements

  1. Demonstrated knowledge of cameras, lenses, and photography accessories
  2. Experience in a customer-facing retail or technical support role
  3. Strong written communication skills for producing product content
  4. Ability to explain technical concepts clearly to non-technical customers
  5. Passion for photography and keeping up with industry trends

Preferable Requirements

  • Prior experience working in a camera or electronics retail environment
  • Knowledge of both mirrorless and DSLR systems across brands (Sony, Canon, Nikon, Fujifilm)
  • Experience with photo editing software such as Adobe Lightroom
  • Portfolio or active photography practice (any genre)
